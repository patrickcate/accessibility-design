--- layout: topic title: "How do I find if a color combination has enough contrast?" id: color-combination category: color ---

With the WCAG standards, color contrast is separated into 3 levels:

Level Small Text Large Text (18 point or 14 point bold)
A 3:1 3:1
AA 4.5:1 3:1
AAA 7:1 4.5:1

The best way however is to use one of the many online tools:

  • WebAIM Constrast Checker
  • Contrast Ratio
  • Color Safe
  • Contrast Checker
